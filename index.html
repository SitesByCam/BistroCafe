My Bistro Café

A cosy place for quality food and coffee

Our bistro café offers a carefully curated menu of freshly prepared breakfasts, light lunches, and homemade pastries, alongside a selection of speciality coffees, teas, and soft drinks. We focus on quality ingredients and simple, well-crafted dishes, with options available throughout the day to suit both quick visits and more relaxed dining. Seasonal items and customer favourites are regularly featured to keep the menu fresh and varied.

Open daily, our café provides a comfortable and welcoming environment for individuals, families, and small groups. Whether you're stopping by for a morning coffee, a casual lunch, or an afternoon break, we aim to deliver friendly service and a consistently enjoyable experience. Takeaway options are also available, making it easy to enjoy our food and drinks on the go.

Outdoor Seating Area

Our outdoor seating area offers a relaxed and inviting space to enjoy the bistro in a more open setting. Surrounded by greenery and soft lighting, it’s perfect for a quiet coffee in the morning or a laid-back meal later in the day.

It’s especially ideal in the warmer months, where the sunlight adds to the atmosphere. Whether you’re enjoying a drink in the afternoon sun or unwinding in the evening with the soft glow of lights around you, it creates a calm and comfortable experience.

Choosing to sit outside lets you enjoy more than just the food — the fresh air, gentle breeze, and natural surroundings make it a great place to meet friends, spend time with family, or simply relax on your own.
